I am a compassionate and experienced Licensed Counsellor (NZAC) and qualified Psychologist trained in Brazil, with over 30 years of diverse experience helping adults navigate life transitions, emotional challenges, and mental health concerns.

I have worked in Brazil, Europe, USA and Australia. Since 2020 I have been part of the health and wellbeing sector in New Zealand.
My professional journey includes both in-person and online counselling, always with a focus on building safe, respectful, and supportive relationships.

I work with people from many different cultural backgrounds, including migrants and those living abroad. I understand the challenges of cultural adaptation, homesickness, and identity.
I also have extensive experience supporting clients dealing with trauma, relationship difficulties, anxiety, depression, grief, and the emotional impact of accidents.

My practice is holistic and person-centred, guided by Carl Rogers’ approach, and enriched by trauma-informed practices, anxiety management strategies, and collaborative goal setting.
I value presence, simplicity, and deep listening, helping clients feel genuinely heard and supported as they move toward growth and wellbeing.

Specializations and Expertises

I am trained and experienced in:

  • Person-Centred Therapy (Carl Rogers) – building trust and authentic connection
  • EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) – for trauma and distressing memories
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT) – working with deep feelings and relationships
  • Calatonia – a gentle somatic-based method for nervous system regulation
  • Systemic Constellations – exploring family and relational dynamics

I bring strong experience working with sensitive issues, including trauma, sexual abuse, domestic violence, neglect, anxiety, and depression. My interventions are safe, trauma-focused, and designed to foster recovery and resilience.

My goal is to promote emotional, cognitive, and behavioural wellbeing, supporting you in developing coping strategies, enhancing daily functioning, and achieving meaningful personal growth
